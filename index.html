Sabaton!

Best Battle History Band out there!

Sabaton is a Swedish Heavy Metal band, that focuses on the history of battles and wars and the stories that come from them. They sing the stories, of the real life wars and teh people who played thier parts in those stories. Gruelling campaigns and awestruck acts of bravery. The magnificent victories and heart pulling personal struggles. The songs tell the true stories, more fantastic than any form of fiction.
